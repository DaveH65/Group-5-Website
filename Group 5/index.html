Online Intrusion

What is Online Intrusions?

It is an unauthorized access someone gains to your computer, typically for nefarious reasons.

Subtopics

Remote Access Trojan

These are used to gain access to someone’s computer.

Botnet

Floods the internet with spam and can be used without the user’s consent.

Pharming

User is directed towards fake websites to extract information from them.

Port Scan

A process of scanning to see how or if ports are being used.

Zero Day Attack

An attack to a system upon the creation of said system, exploitation of vulnerabilities.
